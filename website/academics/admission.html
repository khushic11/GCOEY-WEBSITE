Steps to Follow for Admission:

  1. Fill Out the Online Application Form
    • Access the Application Portal: Visit the official website of the engineering college and navigate to the admissions section.
    • Create an Account: Register with a valid email address and create a password to access the application form.
    • Complete the Form: Fill in all required details, including personal information, educational background, preferred course, and other necessary information.
    • Review and Submit: Carefully review all the entered information for accuracy before submitting the form. Errors can cause delays or rejection.
  2. Submit required documents.
    • Gather Documents: Prepare all necessary documents such as transcripts, certificates, identification proofs, and photographs.
    • Upload Online: Scan and upload the documents in the required format and size to the application portal.
    • Document List:
      • High school transcripts or equivalent
      • Proof of age (birth certificate or national ID)
      • Entrance exam scorecard (if applicable)
      • Passport-sized photographs
      • Proof of residency (if required)
      • Letters of recommendation (if applicable)
      • Personal statement or essay (if required)
  3. Appear for entrance exam (if applicable).
    • Register for Exam: If the college requires an entrance exam, register for the exam through the appropriate portal.
    • Prepare: Study the relevant subjects and topics covered in the exam syllabus. Consider using study guides, taking practice tests, and attending coaching classes if needed.
    • Exam Day: Follow the instructions for the exam day, such as reaching the center on time, carrying necessary identification, and adhering to the exam rules.
    • Results: Wait for the results to be announced. Check the official website or your email for notifications.
  4. Attend counseling sessions.
    • Counseling Schedule: Once shortlisted, you will receive information about the counseling sessions. Note the dates, venues, and any preparatory requirements.
    • Participate in Counseling: Attend the sessions where you will get guidance on selecting your specialization, understanding the curriculum, and other important details.
    • Seat Allotment: Based on your entrance exam score and preferences, seats will be allotted. You may need to choose your branch of engineering during this process.
    • Confirmation: Confirm your acceptance of the seat by completing any required formalities.
  5. Complete fee payment and document verification.
    • Fee Structure: Review the fee structure for the course and understand the payment schedule and methods.
    • Payment: Pay the admission fee through the prescribed mode (online payment, bank transfer, etc.). Keep receipts or proof of payment.
    • Document Verification: Present the original documents for verification as per the collegeâ€™s instructions. This may need to be done in person.
    • Verification Process: College officials will verify your documents to ensure they match the submitted copies.
