Welcome to the College Cooperative Store

Our college cooperative store is a one-stop shop for all your academic and personal needs. We offer a wide range of products including textbooks, stationery, lab equipment, and more.

Managed by the students, for the students, our store aims to provide quality products at reasonable prices. We also offer discounts and special deals throughout the academic year.

Store Timings: Monday to Friday - 9 AM to 5 PM, Saturday - 9 AM to 1 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Location: Ground floor, Block A, near the main entrance.
